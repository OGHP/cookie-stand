Pat's Salmon Cookies

Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies of Seattle! Back in early 2018, Pat had a delicious idea to to create a coffee-time confection by combining two signature Seattle icons: coffee and salmon! They're cookies made into the shape of a salmon and actually made with salmon (though the fish is ground up so fine that you can't even taste it, trust us..they're amazing!). Perfect on a gray Seattle morning or gray Seattle afternoon or gray Seattle evening. You will go gaga for them!
